## 🚀 About Me

**AI Engineer** with a focus on delivering applied AI systems, progressing from foundational machine learning to **production-oriented LLM and Agentic applications**. I specialize in turning AI models into reliable, observable systems that support real operational workflows and reduce manual processing bottlenecks.

- 💼 **Current Focus:** Architecting multi-agent orchestration workflows (LangGraph), self-hosted Vision-Language Models (VLMs), and enterprise RAG applications.
- 🎯 **Engineering Philosophy:** Build robust, human-in-the-loop (HITL) systems with strong observability (Langfuse, MLflow) and measurable business impact.
- 🎓 **Education:** Bachelor of Engineering in Information Technology, VNU-UET.
